Our Story

From Scratch experiments to web-based adventures

How We Started

Whatever Studios began with a simple Scratch game that didn't quite work out. We learned that browser limitations on Scratch prevented us from creating the immersive experiences we envisioned.

Instead of giving up, we pivoted. We discovered modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript) and realized we could create powerful, browser-based games that anyone could play instantly.

Now we're building Wtv Studios from the ground up, focusing on creating accessible, fun, and innovative web games that work on any device.

Our Mission Today

Create engaging web-based games that are free to play, accessible to everyone, and push the boundaries of what's possible in a browser.

Our Journey

From Scratch to Web: The Wtv Studios Story

Early 2023

The Spark of an Idea

It all began with a simple concept: create fun, accessible games that anyone could play. We started experimenting with Scratch, the visual programming platform, thinking it would be the perfect tool for rapid game development.

Spring 2023

First Scratch Game Attempt

We dove into our first project - a multiplayer strategy game on Scratch. Days turned into weeks as we battled with limitations: clunky physics, performance issues, and the realization that browser-based Scratch games couldn't deliver the smooth experience we envisioned.

The Challenge: Scratch's limitations became apparent - limited scalability, performance bottlenecks, and restricted gameplay mechanics.

Summer 2023

The Pivot Point

After months of struggling against Scratch's limitations, we made a tough decision: scrap the project and start fresh. It was a humbling moment, but we learned invaluable lessons about game architecture and performance optimization.

"Sometimes the hardest part of creation is knowing when to start over."
Fall 2023

Web-Based Revolution

We discovered the power of modern web technologies: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and WebGL. The possibilities were endless - real-time multiplayer, 3D graphics, smooth animations, and instant accessibility in any browser.

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript WebGL GitHub Pages
Winter 2023

Whatever Studios is Born

With our new direction clear, we founded Whatever Studios (WtvS). Our mission: create high-quality, browser-based games that push the boundaries of what's possible on the web. No downloads, no installations - just pure, accessible gaming.

Present Day

Building the Future

We're currently developing our first two titles: Domination (turn-based strategy) and Flight Craft (3D aerial combat). Every line of code is written with care, every design decision made with players in mind.

Our Current Focus:

  • Developing scalable game architecture
  • Creating engaging gameplay mechanics
  • Optimizing for all devices and browsers
  • Building a community of players

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide every game we create

Accessibility First

Games should be available to everyone, regardless of device or technical knowledge. No downloads, no installations - just click and play.

Passion-Driven Development

We build games we want to play. Every feature, every mechanic comes from genuine passion for gaming and technology.

Community Focused

Players aren't just consumers - they're collaborators. We listen, adapt, and build games with our community, not just for them.

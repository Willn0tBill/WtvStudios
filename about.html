Our Story

From Scratch to Web Games: The Journey of Wtv Studios

How It All Began

Whatever Studios started with a simple idea: create games that anyone can play in their browser. Our first attempt was a Scratch game that taught us valuable lessons about limitations and possibilities.

The Scratch Experiment

We began by creating a game on Scratch, thinking it would be the perfect platform for quick prototyping. While Scratch is great for learning, we quickly hit its limitations - performance issues, restricted gameplay mechanics, and scalability challenges.

The Realization: We needed more control and power than Scratch could offer.

The Pivot to Web Development

Instead of giving up, we embraced modern web technologies. We discovered that with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, we could create games that:

  • Run in any modern browser
  • Offer smooth 60fps gameplay
  • Support complex game mechanics
  • Work on desktop and mobile
  • Require no downloads or installations
